TIM-Viz displays metabolic pathways selected by the user. It displays reactions as squares that are colored depending on their score. The scores are calculated thanks to gene expression in two conditions in the transcriptomic data file.

The user can choose to also display metabolites. They are represented as white circles. He can also choose to display reactions and metabolites names.
